# DECISIONS LOG
## Major decisions captured from the V2 Super build process

This file records the important decisions made during the architecture and cleanup process.

## Decision 1

Use `ENGRAM_MASTER_v2.md` as the canonical strategic base rather than the older broader master.

Reason:

- cleaner
- less contradictory
- more operational

## Decision 2

Make `V2 Super` a curated system, not a raw mirror.

Reason:

- reduces ambiguity
- keeps the live system clean
- preserves old material in archive without letting it control operations

## Decision 3

Keep the business manual-first.

Reason:

- faster to validate
- avoids premature platform-building
- keeps focus on paid pilots and client proof

## Decision 4

Keep the stack internal in public-facing materials.

Reason:

- trust improves when the offer sounds clear and useful
- overexplaining the stack weakens the pitch
- technical sophistication is support, not headline

## Decision 5

Lock the wedge to beauty and skincare DTC agencies first.

Reason:

- stronger pain
- higher creative velocity
- easier sample audit sourcing
- better fit for the current proof asset

## Decision 6

Adopt the nine-metric ENGRAM vocabulary as the only live audit system.

Reason:

- reduces confusion
- unifies skills, docs, and assets
- avoids drift across old versions

## Decision 7

Use the sample audit as the key proof asset.

Reason:

- strongest credibility tool at this stage
- easiest way to show rather than explain
- highest leverage for outreach and calls

## Decision 8

Add structured assets, not just Markdown docs.

Reason:

- operator kit needs working files
- CSV, YAML, and TXT are better for execution than prose alone

## Decision 9

Create two revenue-core agents:

- Outbound Commander
- Proof Architect

Reason:

- they map directly to the two most important functions right now
- one drives conversations
- one improves trust and proof

## Decision 10

Create support/meta layers rather than treating every agent as equally core.

Support layers include:

- Business Overseer
- Agentic Workflows
- Skill Lab
- design-layer skills

Reason:

- prevents confusion about current priorities
- keeps revenue-focused work at the center

## Decision 11

Add a token-efficiency skill only with strict quality-protection rules.

Reason:

- cost savings matter
- but not at the expense of client-facing quality or strategic clarity

## Decision 12

Push the full bundle to GitHub and add bootstrap files.

Reason:

- continuity across machines
- cleaner restarts in fresh Codex sessions
- reduces dependency on this chat history

## Current standing decision

Do not expand the architecture further until the flagship proof asset is improved and outreach is actually running on real targets.
