07 / Strategic Reconstruction

Strategic Reconstruction

Hook Reframe

Open with a problem, not a product.

Introduce immediate tension with a problem-led entry: “This is why your skin still feels dry after showering.”

Intervention 01
Structural Contrast

Build visible separation between problem and outcome.

Introduce dry vs hydrated contrast to create a clearer attention arc and stronger product consequence.

Intervention 02
Mid-Sequence Interrupt

Break the pattern before attention flattens.

Introduce a texture close-up, camera shift, or sharper claim between seconds 5 and 7 to reset viewer energy.

Intervention 03
Benefit Clarity

Name the outcome without softening it.

State the payoff directly: “Locks moisture into your skin even after rinsing.”

Intervention 04
Closing Pressure

End with a behavioral push, not a fade-out.

Replace the passive ending with a low-friction prompt: “Switch for a week — feel the difference.”
