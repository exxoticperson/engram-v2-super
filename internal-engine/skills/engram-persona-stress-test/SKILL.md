# ENGRAM Persona Stress Test

Use this skill to test whether a message survives contact with real buyer psychology.

## Typical personas

- skeptical agency founder
- busy creative director
- performance operator who wants numbers
- strategist who fears generic tooling
- client-service lead who must explain the spend decision downstream

## For each persona, assess

- what lands immediately
- what sounds vague
- what creates trust
- what creates skepticism
- what objection appears first
- what revision would improve the message

## Best inputs

- pitch line
- offer page
- cold DM
- audit summary
- pricing explanation
- case-study claim

## Rules

- keep personas realistic and commercially literate
- do not make them caricatures
- prioritize objections that could block a sale or weaken credibility

## Output style

This should read like message QA before launch.
