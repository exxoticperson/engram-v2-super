# ENGRAM Positioning

This skill keeps the message sharp and consistent.

## Default framing

ENGRAM is a pre-launch creative diagnostics service for short-form paid-social ads. It shows where the ad loses the viewer, why, and what to change before spend.

## What to optimize for

- clarity
- trust
- premium feel
- operator usefulness

## What to avoid

- stack-led storytelling
- overclaiming
- generic AI-tool language
- drifting beyond the current ICP

## Use this skill for

- one-line positioning
- homepage or deck copy
- FAQ updates
- objection handling
- proof language
- pricing framing
- simplifying internal jargon for external audiences
