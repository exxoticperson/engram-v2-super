# ENGRAM Brand Trust Audit

Use this skill to judge whether ENGRAM feels like a serious premium service.

## Evaluate

- visual professionalism
- clarity of offer
- perceived credibility
- consistency of language
- trust and proof signals
- premium feel versus generic AI-tool feel

## Typical assets

- sample audit PDF
- website or landing page
- proposal deck
- one-pagers
- outreach attachments
- email copy

## Output format

- strongest trust signals
- weakest trust signals
- credibility risks
- premium-positioning gaps
- concrete improvements

## Rules

- do not critique based on taste alone
- tie every finding to trust, clarity, or conversion impact
- keep recommendations specific enough to implement quickly
