ENGRAM CHANNEL PRIORITY MATRIX

Use this order for first-client acquisition.

Tier 1
- warm intros
- referrals
- personalized Loom outreach for highest-fit accounts

Tier 2
- email
- LinkedIn DM

Tier 3
- Instagram DM
- call or voicemail

Tier 4
- public proof posts that support the other channels

How to choose

Founder-led, small agency
- email
- LinkedIn
- call if needed

Creative-first social agency
- Instagram
- LinkedIn
- Loom for top targets

Paid-social operator agency
- email
- LinkedIn
- Loom for top targets

If contact info is weak
- use LinkedIn first
- use referral or warm-intro asks
- use public proof posts to add legitimacy
