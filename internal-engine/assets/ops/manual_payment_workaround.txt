ENGRAM MANUAL PAYMENT WORKAROUND

Default path for first clients
1. send invoice manually
2. collect payment by Wise or direct bank transfer
3. use PayPal only if the deal is otherwise blocked

Rules
- Pilot Audit: 100 percent upfront
- Pre-Flight Audit: 50 percent upfront, 50 percent on delivery
- Retainer: first month upfront

Invoice checklist
- client name
- service name
- amount due
- due date
- Wise link or bank transfer details
- short payment note

Execution rule
- payment does not need a perfect stack right now
- it only needs to not block taking money
