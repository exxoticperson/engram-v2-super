ENGRAM TOKEN EFFICIENCY CHECKLIST

Use this only when quality will not materially drop.

Safe places to save tokens
- batch lead qualification
- first-pass battle cards
- trend clustering
- internal summaries
- file hygiene checks
- structured data updates

Do not save tokens here
- final client-facing audit copy
- flagship sample audit
- final outbound to top-value targets without review
- pricing or core positioning changes
- design direction decisions that affect trust

Questions before optimizing
1. Is this internal-only?
2. Is it easy to verify quickly?
3. Is there a stronger review step after this?
4. Would a weaker result hurt trust, revenue, or clarity?

If yes to question 4, do not optimize.
