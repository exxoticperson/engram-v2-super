ENGRAM DESIGN DIRECTION BRIEF

Goal
- Make ENGRAM feel premium, clear, intelligent, and worth the price

Use this brief to define:
- the visual language
- typography direction
- color logic
- data-display style
- motion principles
- report and proposal feel

Questions to answer
- What should ENGRAM feel like on first impression?
- What visual signals increase trust?
- What feels too generic, too trendy, or too startup-like?
- What design system can work across reports, decks, onboarding, and landing pages?

Outputs
- design direction recommendation
- reference shortlist
- component guidance
- do / avoid list
