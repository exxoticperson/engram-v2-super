# ENGRAM Core Agents
## Revenue-core agents plus one execution coordinator

These are the three agents that matter during the current phase.

## 0. ENGRAM Execution Coordinator

Purpose:

- enforce the first-client sequence
- track readiness
- stop drift back into overbuilding

Best use:

- before sending anything
- while setting up the lead batch
- while clearing payment, intake, and booking blockers
- while reviewing replies and next actions

## 1. ENGRAM Outbound Commander

Purpose:

- move leads through the pipeline
- produce ready outreach
- keep follow-up momentum alive

Best use:

- before outreach
- during weekly pipeline review
- when preparing for calls

## 2. ENGRAM Proof Architect

Purpose:

- strengthen the sample audit
- improve trust and polish
- package proof for outreach and sales

Best use:

- while building the flagship sample audit
- while refining proposals and one-pagers
- after a completed audit or pilot

## How they work together

Outbound Commander feeds:

- target requirements
- objections
- pain signals
- what proof is missing

Proof Architect feeds:

- stronger sample audit
- sharper proof assets
- more credible collateral
- improved trust language

## Shared rule

Every handoff must use `assets/ops/agent_handoff_protocol.txt`.

## Priority

During this phase, these three agents matter more than most other internal architecture. They directly support the only three jobs that matter right now:

- execution discipline
- outreach
- proof
