# ENGRAM — Positioning Document

## What ENGRAM Is

A pre-launch creative diagnostics service for short-form paid-social video ads.

We diagnose where an ad loses the viewer, why it happens, and deliver a concrete repair brief — before media spend.

---

## What ENGRAM Is Not

- Not a creative agency (we don't make ads)
- Not a media buying service (we don't manage spend)
- Not a conversion guarantee (we are decision support, not prophecy)
- Not a dashboard (no SaaS product yet)
- Not a platform (service-first)
- Not an AI chatbot or generic AI tool

That is the only job. Every feature, every metric, every report section serves that job.

**Primary:** Performance creative agencies managing Meta/TikTok paid social for beauty and skincare DTC brands.

**Why this buyer:** They create high volumes of short-form ad creative under constant performance pressure. They feel the pain of creative failure weekly. They already invest in creative intelligence tools. They make fast decisions.

## The Problem We Solve

Agencies and brands currently discover a creative doesn't work after paying to find out — through live media spend. The average bad creative wastes $5,000–$20,000 before being pulled.

The industry has three existing solutions:
1. **Post-hoc analytics** (Motion, Triple Whale) — tells you what happened after spend
2. **Historical benchmarking** (Kantar LINK AI) — compares against test panel norms
3. **Attention heatmaps** (Neurons) — maps visual attention in controlled settings

None of them deliver: diagnosis + cultural context + repair brief + 48-hour turnaround at agency-accessible pricing.

That's the gap ENGRAM fills.
