# ENGRAM — Payment & Contract Templates

---

## PAYMENT SETUP

### Recommended platforms (in order):
1. **Stripe** — Best for global clients, card payments, automatic receipts, professional invoices. Set up at stripe.com. Link to payment page sent after verbal agreement.
2. **Wise** — Best for Gulf/international clients, lower fees on currency conversion, AED-friendly.
3. **PayPal** — Fallback only. Avoid if possible (higher fees, chargeback risk).

### Payment policy:
- **Pilot:** 100% upfront before work begins. No exceptions.
- **Standard audit:** 50% upfront, 50% on delivery.
- **Retainer:** First month upfront, then monthly on the 1st.

### Invoice format:
Use Stripe invoice or a simple PDF with:
- ENGRAM (your name/entity)
- Client name + agency
- Service description: "ENGRAM Pre-Flight Audit — [brand name] — [X] ads"
- Amount
- Due date: Immediate for pilots, NET7 for audits
- Payment link
