ENGRAM MULTI-CHANNEL OUTREACH PLAYBOOK

This is the channel stack for first-client execution.

Rule
- do not rely on one channel
- do not spam every channel at once
- use the same core finding in different surfaces
- the goal is a reply, not a long explanation

PRIORITY ORDER

1. Email
Best for:
- founders
- strategy leads
- paid social leads
- agency operators with visible email addresses

Send:
- curiosity-first opener
- if reply, send compact report
- if engaged, send offer

2. LinkedIn DM
Best for:
- founders
- heads of growth
- paid social leads
- people active on LinkedIn

Send:
- same opener as email, shorter
- no attachment on first touch
- if reply, send compact report or ask where to send it

3. Instagram DM
Best for:
- creative-first agencies
- social-first agencies
- agency founders who are active on brand accounts or personal accounts

Send:
- ultra-short opener
- mention the ad breakdown
- ask if they want it

4. Loom plus email or DM
Best for:
- top 5 to 10 highest-fit targets
- premium agencies
- situations where a written message is not strong enough

Send:
- 30 to 90 second Loom
- one concrete diagnosis
- no platform talk
- link it inside email or DM

5. Cold call
Best for:
- small agencies
- founder-led shops
- when contact surfaces are thin

Use:
- short opener
- ask for the right person
- do not pitch the whole system on the phone

6. Voice note
Best for:
- warmish Instagram or LinkedIn conversations
- founder-led agencies where personality helps

Use:
- one diagnosis
- one reason it matters
- one ask

7. Referral / adjacency asks
Best for:
- operators you already know
- people who do not buy but know agencies

Use:
- "who do you know running paid-social creative for beauty or skincare brands?"

WHAT TO SEND IN EACH CHANNEL

Email
- opener
- compact report after reply
- offer only after engagement

LinkedIn DM
- opener
- compact report after reply

Instagram DM
- opener only
- move to email if they want the report

Loom
- one finding
- one breakpoint
- one fix
- ask if they want the full breakdown

Cold call
- ask who owns launch decisions for creative
- ask if they want the breakdown sent over

Voice note
- use only after a written opener has context

BEST FIRST BATCH MIX

For the first 15 targets:
- 8 email-first
- 4 LinkedIn-first
- 2 Instagram-first
- 1 Loom-assisted

DO NOT DO YET

- SMS blasting
- broad cold calling lists
- paid traffic
- mass automation
- scraping-heavy expansion before the first reply data comes in
