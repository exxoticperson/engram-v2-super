ENGRAM DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT

Discovery
- Walk me through how your team decides which creative goes into spend.
- Where are weak ads slipping through right now?
- When a creative fails, where do you usually find out: hook, middle, or close?
- Do you have any way to diagnose the ad before money is behind it?

Show Phase
- Pull up the sample audit
- Walk one concrete breakpoint
- Explain the repair path in plain language
- Keep the focus on what changes, not on how ENGRAM works

Close
- Does this solve a real problem for your team right now?
- Which current client account is the best fit for a pilot?
- Pilot offer: $2,500 for 1 to 2 ads, compact report, Loom, debrief call, one revision round

Objection Handling
- If they already use another tool: "That is fine. ENGRAM sits earlier. It is for where the ad breaks before spend and what to fix before launch."
- If they ask about accuracy: "The useful part is not pretending to predict outcomes. The useful part is isolating likely weak points before money is behind the cut."
- If they want pricing first: "Pilot is $2,500. The point is to see whether this helps your team catch weak creative earlier and make faster launch calls."

Retainer Trigger
