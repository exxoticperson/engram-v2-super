ENGRAM AGENCY PAIN POINTS

Use these pain points in outreach, calls, Looms, and follow-up.

Core pain points

1. Weak ads still get approved and launched.
2. Teams rely too much on gut feel before spend.
3. Creative feedback is often subjective and hard to operationalize.
4. The ad may look premium but lose attention before the product really lands.
5. Clients want sharper answers for why an ad should or should not launch.
6. Senior strategists do not want to manually break down every cut in depth.
7. Avoidable creative misses waste time, trust, and media budget.
8. Fast-moving teams need a cleaner go / no-go read on priority launches.
9. Agencies want more premium, differentiated client-facing thinking.
10. When something feels off in the edit, teams often cannot pinpoint where it breaks.

Pain points by language angle

Decision pain
- We need a cleaner launch call before money is behind the creative.

Client-management pain
- We need a more concrete explanation for clients than "the hook feels weak."

Creative-ops pain
- We need feedback the creative team can actually use, not vague notes.

Waste pain
- We do not want to spend just to learn the ad had an obvious structural problem.

Positioning pain
- We want to look sharper in front of clients without building another internal process.

Best pain combination for outreach

Use one operational pain plus one client-facing pain.

Example:
- weak ads still get approved
- clients still want a sharper reason for why a cut should change
