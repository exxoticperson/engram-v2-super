ENGRAM OUTREACH SNIPPETS

Rules
- Keep DMs under 80 words
- Do not offer a free audit
- Do not mention internal models, AI, or research tooling
- Lead with the finding, not the system
- Curiosity first, report second, offer third

DM Template A
Hey [Name] - I broke down one of [agency name]'s beauty ads and found exactly where it starts losing attention before the product lands.

Want me to send it over?

DM Template B
Hey [Name] - quick one: I mapped where one of your recent ads drops in the middle and why the close softens before spend.

If you want, I can send the breakdown.

Send-Report Line
Here it is. Pay attention to the first drop and the ending. Those are the two parts worth fixing first.

Offer Line
If useful, I can do this properly on one of your live ads and turn it into a pre-launch repair brief your team can actually use.

Follow-Up
Wanted to bump this once because the breakdown is specific. If the finding feels off, ignore it. If it feels right, I can show you how we'd use this before spend.
