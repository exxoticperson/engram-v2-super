ENGRAM AGENCY POSITIONING ANGLE

What ENGRAM is to agencies

ENGRAM is a specialist pre-launch creative diagnostics layer for agencies running paid social.

It is not there to replace strategy, media buying, or creative direction.

It is there to help agencies:
- catch weak creative before spend
- explain exactly where the ad breaks
- give client-facing justification for what should change
- make faster launch decisions with more confidence

Core line

We help agencies identify where a short-form ad loses attention before spend, why it happens, and what to change before launch.

How to frame the value

Do say:
- extra pre-launch certainty
- cleaner repair path
- outside read on priority launches
- client-facing proof and clarity
- avoided waste from preventable creative misses

Do not say:
- we do your job better than you
- we replace your internal review process
- we predict performance
- we are an AI platform

Best additive framing

Strong agencies use ENGRAM as a pressure-test layer before budget is behind the cut.

That is the pitch.
