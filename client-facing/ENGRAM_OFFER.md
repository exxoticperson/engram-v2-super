# ENGRAM Offer
## Positioning, pricing, and proof policy

## One-sentence positioning

ENGRAM shows where a short-form ad breaks before spend, why it breaks, and what to change before launch.

## Who ENGRAM serves right now

Primary buyer:

- Performance creative agencies
- Managing Meta and TikTok paid social
- Serving beauty and skincare DTC brands

## The problem

Most teams learn a creative is weak only after budget is already running. Existing tools may show performance after the fact or produce scores without a concrete repair path. ENGRAM fills the gap between gut feel and post-hoc analytics.

## Honest competitive framing

- Motion: strong at account-level and post-hoc learning; lighter on diagnosis and repair
- Kantar LINK AI: strong norms and enterprise credibility; expensive and not optimized for agency operators
- Neurons: strong attention and memory framing; lighter on current category context and hands-on repair
- ENGRAM: diagnosis, repair brief, current category context, and a human walkthrough in 48 to 72 hours

## Offer ladder

Use this exact ladder everywhere:

| Offer | Price | Included |
|-------|-------|----------|
| Pilot Audit | $2,500 | 1 to 2 ads, full report, 30-minute debrief |
| Pre-Flight Audit | $5,000 to $7,500 | 3 to 5 ads, full reports, debrief, recut briefs |
| Pre-Production Screen | $2,000 | Script, storyboard, rough cut, or brief review before production |
| Monthly Retainer | $10,000 to $15,000 | Ongoing pre-launch scoring, monthly trend context, priority turnaround |

## Pricing posture

- Do not discount early
- Do not offer a free audit in cold outreach
- Do offer the sample audit as a proof asset
- Refund language is acceptable for the pilot if confidence needs to be signaled

## Why the price is justified

An agency pushing spend behind the wrong creative can waste thousands of dollars just to learn a predictable structural failure. ENGRAM is priced against avoided waste and faster launch decisions, not analyst hours.

## Public proof policy

Use this exact statement in every client-facing report and in any trust-building material:

> ENGRAM diagnostics are based on neural response prediction and semantic analysis signals. They identify likely attention failures and comprehension gaps. They are not guarantees of business outcomes. This is a decision-support tool designed to improve launch selection and reduce creative waste, not a predictor of conversion rate, ROAS, or sales.

## What we do not say in public

- No model names
- No "brain AI"
- No "fMRI startup"
- No "guaranteed improvement"
- No "we are the first"

## What we do say in public

- Where the creative loses the viewer
- Why it happens
- What to change
- How quickly the audit is delivered
- Why agencies use it before launch
