# ENGRAM Pilot
## Pre-launch creative diagnostics for paid-social agencies

## What it is

ENGRAM shows where a short-form ad loses attention before spend, why it happens, and what to change before launch.

This is not post-launch reporting.

This is a pre-launch decision and repair layer.

## Best fit right now

- performance creative agencies
- Meta and TikTok paid social
- beauty and skincare DTC brands

## What the pilot includes

- 1 to 2 ads
- one compact diagnostic report per ad
- optional Loom walkthrough
- one debrief call
- one revision round

## What the client sees

- attention curve
- performance breakdown
- top breakpoints
- top fixes
- launch recommendation

## Turnaround

- 48 hours from receipt of all required inputs

## Investment

- $2,500 upfront

## Positioning line

Use this exact line:

> We run pre-launch creative diagnostics. We identify where ads lose attention before spend and what is worth fixing before launch.

## Proof boundary

> ENGRAM diagnostics identify likely attention failures and comprehension gaps before spend. They are a decision-support tool designed to improve launch choices and reduce creative waste. They are not guarantees of conversion rate, ROAS, or sales.

## Why this matters

Most teams only learn a creative is weak after money is already behind it.

ENGRAM helps teams catch the weak point earlier, fix faster, and launch with more conviction.
