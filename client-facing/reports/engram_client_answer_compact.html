06 / Recommended Changes
Client Answer
Change 01

Open on the skin problem, not the bottle. Make the first second feel necessary.

Change 02

Add contrast in the middle. Do not let every frame feel equally calm.

Change 03

End with a reason to act now. The close should move behavior, not just describe benefit.

The ad has premium ingredients. It needs sharper pressure.
