# ENGRAM FAQ
## Trust, objections, and explanation language

## What is ENGRAM in plain language?

ENGRAM is a pre-launch creative diagnostics service for short-form paid-social ads. It shows where an ad loses the viewer, why that happens, and what to fix before spend.

## Are you predicting ROAS or conversion rate?

No. ENGRAM is a decision-support system. It helps teams reduce creative waste and make better launch choices. It does not guarantee business outcomes.

## How is this different from Motion, Kantar, or Neurons?

ENGRAM is designed to deliver a fast diagnosis and a repair path for agencies before launch. The value is not just a score. The value is the combination of:

- clear breakdown
- repair recommendations
- current category context
- a human debrief

## What does the client actually receive?

- one PDF audit per creative
- optional Loom walkthrough
- live debrief call

## Why should anyone trust the audit?

Because the report is concrete, timestamped, and action-oriented. It does not ask the client to believe hype. It shows what is happening in the creative, where the likely failure points are, and what changes are worth testing.

## What if the client asks how the analysis works?

Use plain language:

> We combine structured creative analysis, semantic interpretation, and response modeling to identify likely attention and comprehension issues. The output matters more than the mechanics: these are the moments where the ad is likely losing the viewer and the edits most worth testing before launch.

## What should never be said?

- We guarantee better results
- We know exactly how the ad will perform
- We are reading the viewer's brain
- We are the first company to do this
- The model is the differentiator

## What is the best proof asset?

The sample audit. It should be the first thing shown in outreach, on calls, and in follow-up.

## What happens after the first paid pilot?

- deliver fast
- debrief clearly
- log what mattered most to the client
- follow up on implementation and outcomes
- use the best insight as the next sales asset
