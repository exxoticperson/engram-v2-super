# ENGRAM Core
## Canonical doctrine for the live business

ENGRAM is a pre-launch creative diagnostics service for short-form paid-social video ads.

Its job is simple:

> Show where a short-form ad loses the viewer, explain why, and hand back the specific fix before media spend.

This is the only live version of the business inside `V2 Super`.

## What ENGRAM is

- A manual service first
- A decision-support system for launch selection
- A diagnostic and repair layer for short-form ad creative
- A premium operator workflow for agencies under creative-performance pressure

## What ENGRAM is not

- Not a conversion guarantee
- Not a ROAS predictor
- Not a dashboard
- Not a SaaS platform yet
- Not an automated video generation service
- Not a neuroscience brand in public

## Current wedge

ENGRAM starts with performance creative agencies managing Meta and TikTok paid social for beauty and skincare DTC brands.

Why this wedge:

- High creative velocity
- High sensitivity to hook quality and reveal timing
- Existing tool budget
- Fast feedback loops
- Clear pain around pushing budget behind the wrong cut

Secondary category after validation:

- Fashion and apparel DTC agencies

Not now:

- Direct brands as the primary GTM
- Enterprise procurement-heavy accounts
- Gulf market as the main campaign
- Pharma, EdTech, architecture, SaaS, and long-form-first categories

## Core promise boundary

ENGRAM does not predict business outcomes. It surfaces likely attention failures, comprehension gaps, structural weaknesses, and repair opportunities so a buyer can make a better launch decision.

Use this exact stance everywhere:

> ENGRAM diagnostics are based on neural response prediction and semantic analysis signals. They identify likely attention failures and comprehension gaps. They are not guarantees of business outcomes. This is a decision-support tool designed to improve launch selection and reduce creative waste, not a predictor of conversion rate, ROAS, or sales.

## Client deliverable

The live offer is a manual service with three parts:

- ENGRAM Pre-Flight Audit PDF
- Optional Loom walkthrough
- Live debrief call

Nothing in the live bundle should imply more automation than that.

## Locked metric vocabulary

These nine metrics are the only live audit vocabulary:

1. Hook Friction
2. Comprehension Drift
3. Reveal Waste
4. CTA Recall Gap
5. Modality Conflict
6. Branding Delay
7. Edit Saturation
8. Dead Air Window
9. Cultural-Neural Alignment

Do not use any surviving eight-metric system in active docs or skills.

## Public narrative rules

- Do not name internal models in public-facing copy
- Do not say ENGRAM is first
- Do not promise better performance outcomes
- Do not lead with the stack
- Do lead with diagnosis, repair, speed, and avoided waste

## Internal stance on the stack

The stack exists to improve analyst output. It is not the product headline.

Internal tools may support:

- response modeling
- semantic mapping
- trend research
- rewrite and repair drafting

Publicly, ENGRAM sells clarity and action.

## Validation rule

ENGRAM is being operated as a 14-day validation sprint, not as a software startup.

Success at this phase means:

- qualified outreach sent
- replies
- calls
- one paid pilot

If the message fails, fix the message before building software.
