# ENGRAM Ad Spy

Use this skill to study active creative in the market without drifting into vanity research.

## Sources

- Meta Ad Library
- TikTok Creative Center
- visible campaign landing pages
- public creative repositories when relevant

## What to capture

- hook structure
- reveal timing
- offer framing
- CTA structure
- pacing pattern
- visual treatment
- variant volume

## What to conclude

For each competitor or category set:

- what they are testing hard
- what pattern they seem to believe in
- where fatigue may be setting in
- where ENGRAM could challenge or validate that direction

## Best uses

- sample audit sourcing
- outreach personalization
- category benchmark preparation
- competitor battle cards

## Rules

- do not confuse visibility with performance certainty
- use ad longevity and pattern repetition as clues, not proof
- capture only insights that can influence a sales or audit decision
