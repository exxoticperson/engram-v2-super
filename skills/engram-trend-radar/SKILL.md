# ENGRAM Trend Radar

Use this skill to identify trends that matter commercially.

## Focus

Not every trend matters. Prioritize trends that change:

- how creatives are opened
- how quickly products are revealed
- how buyers interpret current category norms
- what agencies are suddenly under pressure to solve

## Output priorities

For each trend, answer:

1. What is changing
2. Why it matters now
3. Who cares first
4. How ENGRAM can use it
5. Whether it belongs in outreach, content, or an audit

## Categories of trends

- creative format shifts
- category messaging shifts
- buyer expectation shifts
- tool-market shifts
- platform behavior shifts

## Rules

- prefer directional signals over hype
- do not present weak signals as certainty
- separate "interesting" from "commercially useful"
- tie the trend back to ENGRAM's wedge whenever possible

## Output style

Trend radar should read like strategic intelligence for an operator, not a generic social trends roundup.
