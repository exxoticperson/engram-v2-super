# ENGRAM Sales Call

This skill governs live sales conversations.

Use `assets/sales/discovery_call_script.txt` as the default call sheet.

## Call structure

### Discovery

Ask:

- how they currently decide what goes into spend
- what their testing process looks like
- what creative failure hurt most recently
- whether they have a way to diagnose the ad before launch

Listen first. Do not pitch too early.

### Show phase

- show the sample audit
- walk one concrete diagnosis
- explain the repair path
- keep methodology language plain

### Close phase

- confirm whether the problem is real
- identify which current account is the best fit
- offer the pilot at $2,500
- explain timing, deliverables, and next step

## Retainer trigger

After repeat paid usage:

> Based on how your team is using this, the most efficient setup is to make ENGRAM part of your launch process. Every ad gets scored before it goes live, monthly trend context is included, and turnaround stays fast. If useful, I can put that into a monthly scope for you.

## Rules

- do not oversell the stack
- do not offer free audits
- do not negotiate against yourself
- use the sample audit as the proof asset
