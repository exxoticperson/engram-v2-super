# ENGRAM Outreach

This is the live outreach playbook for ENGRAM.

Use `assets/sales/outreach_snippets.txt` as the reusable swipe file.

For stage-aware outbound email sequencing and follow-up logic, use `skills/engram-email-automation/SKILL.md`.

## Locked ICP

Primary:

- performance creative agencies
- running Meta and TikTok paid social
- serving beauty and skincare DTC brands

Secondary after early validation:

- fashion and apparel DTC agencies

Not yet:

- direct brand-led GTM
- enterprise accounts
- Gulf-first campaign
- unrelated verticals

## Qualification signals

Positive signals:

- agency uses performance creative language
- team talks about hook rate, testing, creative fatigue, hold rate, or iteration
- team uses creative intelligence tools
- visible beauty or skincare client work
- team size large enough to buy premium services and move quickly

Negative signals:

- pure media buying
- no creative involvement
- enterprise-only positioning
- no evidence of paid social work

## Lead list fields

- first name
- last name
- title
- agency
- LinkedIn URL
- email if available
- client category
- spend tier estimate
- pain signal
- personalization hook

## Outreach rules

- keep DMs under 100 words
- no internal model names
- no "neural" language
- no "free audit" language
- no guarantees
- one specific observation
- one clear value statement
- one low-friction ask

## Core DM template

```
Hey [Name] - noticed you've been talking about [creative fatigue / hook rates / testing volume].

We run pre-launch diagnostics on short-form ads for Meta and TikTok. We flag where a creative loses the viewer, why it happens, and what is worth fixing before media spend.

For beauty and skincare especially, that first hook window is brutal.

Want me to send the sample audit so you can see the format?
```

## Tool-aware template

```
Hey [Name] - saw [agency name] is already using tools like [tool name] for creative analysis.

ENGRAM covers the pre-launch side: where the ad breaks, why, and what to recut before it goes live.

Happy to send the sample audit if useful.
```

## Follow-up template

```
Hey [Name] - following up on the creative audit note.

I built a sample audit on a beauty ad recently. It shows exactly how we break down the hook, reveal, and repair path before launch.

If you want, I can send it over.
```

## Pricing posture

- do not negotiate price before they understand the deliverable
- do not offer a free audit
- the pilot is $2,500
- the sample audit is the proof asset

## Close objective

The job of outreach is not to explain the entire stack. The job is to earn a call or permission to send the sample audit.
