# ENGRAM Offer Gap Research

Use this skill to spot where ENGRAM can be more precise, more premium, or more necessary.

## Use cases

- sharpening the initial wedge
- testing adjacent verticals after validation
- identifying underserved offer formats
- deciding what not to build yet

## Research questions

- what existing tools leave unresolved
- which buyer segment feels the pain most acutely
- which offer framing competitors ignore
- where speed or human diagnosis is more valuable than dashboards
- what high-margin sub-problem could become a later SKU

## Output format

For each gap:

- gap statement
- who feels it
- why it persists
- why ENGRAM could credibly fill it
- whether it is a now, later, or never move

## Rules

- stay commercially grounded
- do not recommend expanding the offer just because a gap exists
- rank gaps by pain, urgency, credibility, and speed to sell
